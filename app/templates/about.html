About GraphRAG

Building the future of intelligent knowledge management

Our Mission

At GraphRAG, we believe that knowledge should be accessible, interconnected, and intelligent. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to transform their unstructured documents into powerful, queryable knowledge graphs that reveal hidden insights and connections.

By combining the power of graph databases with advanced AI language models, we're making it possible for anyone to build sophisticated knowledge systems without deep technical expertise.

What We Do

AI-Powered Entity Extraction

Automatically identify and extract entities, relationships, and concepts from your documents using state-of-the-art language models.

Interactive Graph Visualization

Explore your knowledge in a visual, intuitive interface that reveals connections and patterns you might have missed.

Natural Language Queries

Ask questions in plain English and get intelligent answers powered by your knowledge graph and AI.

Embeddable Widgets

Share your knowledge graphs with customers or team members through easy-to-embed chatbot widgets.
